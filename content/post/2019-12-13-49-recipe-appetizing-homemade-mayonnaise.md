### Ingredients of Homemade Mayonnaise

  1. You need 2 of egg yolks. 
  2. You need 1 tbsp of mustard. 
  3. You need 1 1/4 cup of oil (use flavorless oil like sunflower seeds or canola). 
  4. You need 1 tsp of salt. 
  5. Prepare 1/2 tsp of vinegar. 
  6. It&#8217;s of Juice from 1/2 lemon. 
  7. Prepare 2 tsp of sugar. 
  8. You need 2 tbsp of honey. 

### Homemade Mayonnaise instructions

  1. Whisk egg yolks with mustard. When the mixture becomes thick, add 1/4 cup of oil, pouring slowly while whisking.. 
  2. Add salt, vinegar, lemon, sugar, honey. Whisk again.. 
  3. Whisk while pouring the rest of the oil slowly. Adjust the taste if necessary..
