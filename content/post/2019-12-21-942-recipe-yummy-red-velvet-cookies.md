**Red Velvet Cookies**.

### Ingredients of Red Velvet Cookies

  1. It&#8217;s 1 Box (15.25 oz) of Red Velvet cake mix (I like the mix w/pudding). 
  2. You need 1/2 Cup of Vegetable oil. 
  3. It&#8217;s 2 of Large eggs. 
  4. You need 1 1/4 Cup of white chocolate chips. 

### Red Velvet Cookies step by step

  1. Combine cake mix, oil, & eggs. Mix well.. 
  2. Mix in chocolate chips. 
  3. Cover bowl in plastic wrap and refrigerate for 45 mins. 
  4. Roll in balls, about 2 tbsp. Arrange on baking sheet about 2 inches apart.. 
  5. Bake at 350 for about 13 mins each or until crisp around edges..
