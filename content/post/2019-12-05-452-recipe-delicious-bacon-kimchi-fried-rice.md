  2. You need 8 pcs of bacon, cut into small pieces. 
  3. It&#8217;s 3 cloves of garlic, minced. 
  4. It&#8217;s 150 gr of kimchi, cut into small pieces. 
  5. You need 2 tbsp of soy sauce.
