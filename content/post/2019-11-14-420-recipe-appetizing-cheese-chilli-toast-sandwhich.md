### Ingredients of Cheese Chilli Toast Sandwhich

  1. You need 8 of White / Brown / Multigrain Bread Slices. 
  2. Prepare 1 cup of Grated Cheese. 
  3. You need 1/2 cup of Finely Chopped Green Chillies. 
  4. It&#8217;s 1/2 tsp of Chilli Flakes. 
  5. It&#8217;s 1/2 tsp of Oregano. 
  6. You need 1/4 tsp of Salt. 
  7. It&#8217;s of Butter as required. 

### Cheese Chilli Toast Sandwhich instructions

  1. In a mixing bowl, add 1 cup Grated Cheese (Any of your choice) followed by ½ cup of finely chopped Green Chillies.. 
  2. Then add ½ tsp of Chilli Flakes, ½ tsp of Oregano and ¼ tsp of Salt mix well and keep aside.. 
  3. On preheated tawa take 1 tsp of Butter and grease it well.. 
  4. Take two slices of Bread, roast for 1 Min from one side and flip the Bread slice.. 
  5. Add 1 ½ tsp of Cheese stuffing on both the slices.. 
  6. Cover with the lid and simmer on low flame until turns crispy.. 
  7. In a similar way, make all the Bread toast.. 
  8. When turns crispy remove into the serving, dish and cut diagonally and serve hot with Tomato Sauce..
