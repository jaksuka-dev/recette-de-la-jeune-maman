**Cabbage, Smoked Sausage, and Carrot**.

Here is how you achieve that. 

### Ingredients of Cabbage, Smoked Sausage, and Carrot

  1. It&#8217;s 2 cups of bias sliced carrots. 
  2. It&#8217;s 1/2 head of green cabbage. 
  3. You need 1 pound of smoked sausage. 
  4. You need 1 cup of diced onion. 
  5. Prepare 1/4 cup of duck fat. 
  6. You need To taste of salt. 
  7. You need 1 cup of beef broth. 
  8. You need To taste of your favorite hot sauce optional. 

### Cabbage, Smoked Sausage, and Carrot step by step

  1. Peel and slice the carrots on a bias. Dice the onions. Slice the cabbage. Slice the sausage on a bias.. 
  2. Heat the duck fat. Add the onions sauté till translucent. Add the sausage.. 
  3. Add all the remaining ingredients. Except the beef broth. Stir often and cover. Cook for 30 minutes.. 
  4. Add beef broth and allow to be absorbed. When absorbed it's ready serve. Add hot sauce if you like. I hope you enjoy!!!.
