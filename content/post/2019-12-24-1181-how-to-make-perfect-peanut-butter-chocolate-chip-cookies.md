### Ingredients of Peanut Butter Chocolate Chip Cookies

  1. Prepare 1 cup of Butter. 
  2. You need 1 cup of Peanut Butter. 
  3. Prepare 2 cups of Sugar. 
  4. You need 2 of Eggs. 
  5. You need 2 cups of Flour. 
  6. Prepare 1 tsp of Baking Soda. 
  7. It&#8217;s 2 cups of Chocolate Chips. 

Add peanut butter and both sugars and beat until combined well. Stir in flour and baking soda gradually until well combined. Stir in chocolate chips by hand. Using a meatball scooper or a spoon, scoop out small portions of dough and drop onto parchment lined cookie sheets. 

### Peanut Butter Chocolate Chip Cookies step by step

  1. Mix sugar, eggs, and melted butter in a bowl. Add flour and baking soda. Then add in peanut butter until fully mixed. Lastly mix in the chocolate chips.. 
  2. Portion cookies onto a cookie sheet.. 
  3. Preheat oven to 350°F. Bake for 12 minutes..
